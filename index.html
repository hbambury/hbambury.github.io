About me

I am a PhD student at Ecole Normale Superieure and Inria under the supervision of Phong Nguyen. My research has to do with the use of number theory and algorithms in post-quantum cryptography. Currently, I am interested in algorithms that find short vectors in ideal lattices, and in the distribution of these ideal lattices.
